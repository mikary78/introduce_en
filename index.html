About Parandurume Inc.

Parandurume Inc. is an Extended Reality (XR) technology startup founded in 2019, headquartered at Gwangmyeong Startup Support Center in Gyeonggi Province.
The company name 'Parandurume' means *"a crane spreading its wings to bring brilliant solutions to your business and work"*,
embodying our vision to bring positive change to the world through cutting-edge technology.

Parandurume has a philosophy of creating an inclusive and prosperous world where everyone can enjoy the benefits of exponentially advancing technology.
Under this philosophy, Parandurume was designated as a Preliminary Social Enterprise in Gyeonggi Province in July 2025, officially recognizing our commitment to creating social value.

Core Technology & Products: XR Without Headsets

Parandurume breaks the stereotype of XR requiring massive headsets (HMDs),
implementing a 3D XR environment where reality and virtual merge by attaching small accessory devices to existing desktops or laptops.

Parandurume XR Kit Demo Video

Demo video showing the actual XR kit operation and key features.

Parandurume XR Kit

This Minimum Viable Product (MVP) concentrates our technology, enabling web-based XR content with simple peripherals. When connected to a PC, users can directly manipulate 3D virtual objects within the monitor screen or project them into real space as augmented reality (AR).

Based on Leonar3Do, a PC-based VR device developed by Hungarian CTO Daniel Balazs Ratai, Parandurume is advancing this technology to expand into a universal XR platform usable in education, medical, and training fields without separate HMDs. We're also integrating with cutting-edge AI technologies like Azure AI, building an environment where anyone can run XR applications with just a web browser.
